I’m a Research Engineer at New York University Abu Dhabi. I work on building research tooling and infrastructure, with a focus on LLM evaluation, uncertainty, and data collection pipelines.

Previously, I worked on software development projects across The Gambia, Ghana, Rwanda, and the US, and I was a graduate teaching assistant at Carnegie Mellon University Africa.

Current interests:

- Agentic evaluation and critique systems
- Data-voids, overconfidence, and misinformation risks in LLM reasoning
- Building research infrastructure
